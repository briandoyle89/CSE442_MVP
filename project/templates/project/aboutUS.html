Sharing is Learning


UB_Liberator is built to help students share learning resources. You can download old hws, exams, code etc for CSE courses at UB. Furthermore you can upload similar files to share with other students.

Project Description

From the Developers of UB_Liberator, bits_please-

Our website UB_Liberator is designed to allow students, specifically CSE students at UB, to have access to another resource for class notes and materials they may have lost or not completed correctly. Through being able to search for files, and rate them through an upvote/downvote system, students can find the information they need to succeed to the best of their capabilities. Files uploaded by users are their own works and any transgressions of academic integrity or other code of conduct is not the fault of the developers, bits_please.

Description for Users

Please follow this link to our website-
https://ubliberator3.pythonanywhere.com/login_user?next=/course_list . Upon creating an account, you will be taken to the main Dashboard page of our site, where you can begin to find the documents and pages you need to do the best that you can in your courses! The left side of the page holds the tools you need to browse through our current course list, view your previous downloads and uploads, and upload a document.

How to submit bug report

If you experience any troubles while using our site, bugs can be reported at our GitHub repository at https://github.com/briandoyle89/CSE442_MVP/issues . The more information you can provide about the problems you encounter, the better we can solve them and create a more positive user experience for all users.

Instructions suitable for a non-technical user(For the needy)

"Project Description" provided in minute details
To Register:
Press the button on the left side of the screen “Don’t Have an Account? Click Here.” to be taken to a screen where a valid format of email, a username that hasn’t been taken yet, and a password that is not blank, can be entered into their corresponding boxes to the left of their texts (Email, Username, and Password) and after using the keyboard to type your responses and clicking submit, your account is created.

To Download:
After logging into the site, you can view on the dashboard the classes CSE115 and CSE442, and their corresponding buttons “Choose This Class” below them. By clicking on their respective “Choose This Class” buttons, the list of materials for the course available for download can be seen. Then, by navigating down the list of file names and their respective “Download this File” buttons, files can be found, and clicking the “Download this File” button, begins the download.

To Upload:
After logging into the site, and being taken to the Dashboard automatically, you can click on the button on the left navigation bar titled “Upload a DOCUMENT” and after clicking into the box to the right of “File name:” and entering information for a file name, choosing a course, and choosing a file, one can click upload to upload the file to the specified course.

To Search Files By Class:
Once you have logged in and are taken to the Dashboard, you can select from a drop down menu button labelled Select Course, the course you wants the study materials for. Once you’ve selected the course and clicked the search button, all the files associated with that course will be populated on the screen, and can then be downloaded.
